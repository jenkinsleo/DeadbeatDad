Thank you for getting this tileset! I hope it can be useful for you and for your projects!

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The size of this tileset is 8px x 8 px. 
The base tilesets of this Small City Extra tileset are https://marceles.itch.io/citytileset8px and https://marceles.itch.io/assets-for-8px-top-down-games which are free. This tileset works as an extension of those tiles, with more buildings, roads and cars.

"Small city tilesets 8px" is a work in progress asset project, so my main goal is to create a lot of tilesets, sprites, UI tiles that can be use as a robust and complete tileset. And so, if you have any special request or are in need of any special asset feel free to let me know on my itch.io https://marceles.itch.io/ or as a mail on fakegamedevplus@gmail.com

This Small City Extra tileset unlike the previous 2 tilesets, only uses one color palette, included, which is the Pico-8 secret palette https://lospec.com/palette-list/pico-8-secret-palette.

For commissions i'm always available. O(∩_∩)O
